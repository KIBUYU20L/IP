Great Family Childrens Home

Make a difference in the lives of orphaned kids in Africa.

Vission of G.F.C.H

Empower a vulnerable or orphan child in Kenya, Africa to live a full and productive life through the provision of education, shelter, and medical care in a caring and loving environment.

The children are taught Christian values. Even though there is no religious discrimination within G.F.C.H, children who come to the home are taught to live a Christian life.G.F.C.H participate in Sunday school activities including annual festivals. G.F.C.H children know to greet all their visitors who come into the home with angelic voices- singing gospel songs as a sign of appreciation.

Mission of G.F.C.H

To Provide Quality Child-Centered Programs That Prepare And Empower Vulnerable Children And Families For A Bright Future.

Due to the COVID-19 pandemic,Great Family Childrens Home is currently facing some challenges and we need your SUPPORT. Great Family Kids, Inc. Board of Directors postponed our annual “Make A Dream Come True” Gala that was scheduled for September 4th, 2020 as a precaution. During this campaign, our goal is to buy enough food supplies for 3 months since the food prices have skyrocketed due to supply interruption.
